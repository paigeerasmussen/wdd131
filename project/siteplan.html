Cat Food Comparisons

Site Name

The site domain name is Cat Food Comparisons or catfoodcomparisons.com. This name describes the simple purpose of the website and is easy to understand.

Site Purpose

The purpose is to compare popular cat food brands to make it easier for consumers to pick a cat food brand that works best for them.

Scenarios

- What is the best cat food for my cat?

- How can I make sure they are getting the nutrition they need?

Color Schema

The colors for this website are Apricot Cream and Muted Olive. Orange is lively and full of personality like cats, and green shows health and makes people think of healthy eating. The hex codes are #FFC685 and #A7C064.

Fonts

The fonts for this page are Comforta and Roboto. Roboto will be for the main text. Comforta is a decorative font and is used for the Header and other accents.
